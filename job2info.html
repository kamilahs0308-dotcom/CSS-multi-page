AI Engineer

The artificial intelligence engineer role is an advanced progression from the software developer/engineer position. As an AI engineer, you will craft and define the architecture for AI software and applications projects. You will oversee the entire software development process and may manage projects for various clients. By analyzing customer or user needs, you will define system architecture and lead a team of software developers to design programs and build applications.
